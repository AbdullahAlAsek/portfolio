Overview

About Me

DAAD Scholar | Studying MSc in LENC at Universität Greifswald, Germany.

Passionate nature conservationist with over three years of experience in biodiversity research, ecological restoration, and sustainable ecosystem management. Currently pursuing an MSc in Landscape Ecology and Nature Conservation at Universität Greifswald as a prestigious DAAD scholarship recipient.

Bringing a strong interdisciplinary foundation in zoology, I am specializing in conservation genetics, bioinformatic tools, and genomic data analysis to drive data-informed protection of endangered species.

Skills & Expertise

  • Genetics & Bioinformatics: Genomic Data Analysis, Genetic Conservation, Whole-Genome Sequencing (WGS), Raw genomic data analysis, read quality checking (FastQC), k-mer analysis (Kmergenie), assembly validation (QUAST), De novo sequence assembly preparation using pipelines (ABySS, Platanus, SOAPdenovo2) and mitochondrial genome assembly (NOVOPlasty), sequence analysis (MITOS), and repeat content annotation (RepeatMasker), Evolutionary and phylogenetic analysis (MEGA X), comparative genome analyses (OrthoVenn2), and genomic visualization (OGDRAW), DNA extraction, genetic bird sex identification.
  • Conservation & Field Ecology: Biodiversity Assessments, Limnology, Water Quality Analysis, Animal Behavior Observation, Radio Telemetry, Ecological Restoration Planning.
  • Data Science & Programming: R Programming, Python, Linux command line, Multivariate Statistics, Principal Component Analysis (PCA), Data Visualization (ggplot2).
  • Spatial Analysis & Ecological Modeling: GIS Mapping (QGIS, ArcGIS), Remote Sensing (EnMAP-Box), Supervised and unsupervised classification in the EnMAP-Box, Land Cover Change Detection.
  • Software & Image Processing: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Microsoft Word, Excel, Powerpoint, Picasa, GIMP.
